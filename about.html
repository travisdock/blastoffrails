Why?

I used to consistently go to two Rails conferences that are no longer happening. Conference organizing is a difficult and sometimes expensive endeavor and not all conferences can go on forever. I decided to start my own because I really want there to be more places for Rails developers to gather. I get so much energy and inspiration from attending conferences and I want more of that.

The Blog

I am also publishing a blog about the process of organizing this conference with video interviews of other conference organizers and weekly updates on my progress. Check it out if you are interested in how this conference is coming together behind the scenes.
